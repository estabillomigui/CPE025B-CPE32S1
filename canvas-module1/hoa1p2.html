Huevos Rancheros

A classic huevos rancheros recipe featuring fresh pico de gallo on top. Huevos rancheros is a vegetarian Mexican breakfast with eggs, tortillas and salsa.

Ingredients:

  • Pico De Gallo around 2 cups
  • Refried Beans 1 can
  • Corn Tortillas 4 pieces
  • eggs 4 pieces

Preparation:

  1. Prepare the pico de gallo
  2. Cook the beans
  3. Add extra seasoning
  4. Heat salsa and tortilla in separate pans
  5. Fry the eggs
  6. Assemble the meal
  7. Add additional salsa

Extra Notes:

  • If tomatos aren't in season, you can skip the pico de gallo
  • You can modify the recipe however you want
